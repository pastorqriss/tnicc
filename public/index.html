WELCOME TO CORBON

The Council of Registered Builders of Nigeria, CORBON was established by Federal Republic of Nigeria Act Cap No. 40(formerly known as Decree No. 45 of 1989), and now established by BUILDERS REGISTRATION ACT CAP. B13LFN 2004. The Council, as defined in Section 2 of the law, is a body corporate empowered to regulate the practice of building construction, maintenance, and management in Nigeria. Regulating and controlling the practice of Building profession in all its aspects and ramifications. Conducting Professional Accreditation of Tertiary Academic Institutions and training facilities for the Building profession (Universities & Polytechnics). Conducting professional examinations/interviews for those who are aspiring to qualify to practice the profession in Nigeria. Maintenance of discipline within the profession. Registration of would-be professionals. Providing Continuing Professional Development for the registered persons. Performing such other functions conferred on the Council by the enabling law.

N-POWER BUILD PROGRAMME 2019

The N-power Build is an accelerated training and certification program initiated and sponsored by the Federal Government, geared towards delivering a crop of skilled and highly competent workforce of technicians, artisans and service professionals. The present life-changing opportunity for you and millions of our citizens. The training is tailored to the National Occupation Standard (NOS) for building construction trades and assessment / certification is evidence based in line with the National Skills Qualification (NSQ) Framework. The N-Power Build program is anchored on apprentice (on the Job training) with a blend of formal and informal training.
