Creating more smiles!

While we are opportuned to receive Rhapsody of Realities online, there are so many people in countries with no internet, no roads or even electricity. Their only hope of ever receiving the word of God is through the printed copies of Rhapsody of Realities. We have had teams who had to travel by local boats and in some places have had to walk by foot for 24 hours just to get them copies of Rhapsody of Realities.

Cases where the villagers practically ran after copies of the messenger Angel. Communities where 2 or more houses had to share 1 copy. The people yearn for the word of God embedded in Rhapsody of Realities. Your gift of 1 dollar will help us reach more people in such communities, if Rhapsody has been blessing you, then make it available for others in the language they understand best.
